Coinomi currently supports Bitcoin, Dogecoin, Litecoin, Peercoin, Darkcoin, Reddcoin, NuBits, NuShares, Blackcoin, Feathercoin, Rubycoin, Digitalcoin, and Cannacoin . More to follow soon!

Universal Wallet

Now you have the ability to control all of your coins through a single app. Effortless connection with any part of the world, simply imagine Economy 2.0. No more confusion and hassle!

Top Security

Your private keys never leave your device. We are using strong public cryptography and the best industry practices. We make sure that your money is totally safe by ensuring that you have the ultimate control over it!

Convenient Backup

All your coins are accessed with one Hierarchical Deterministic Wallet (BIP44), so instead of having to regularly back-up every wallet for every single coin, all you have to do is keep a single master key in a safe place (even on a piece of paper). Never lose your funds ever again!
