Crypto currencies made easy

Access all coins

Now you have the ability to control all your coins through one app. Effortless connection with any part of the world, simply imagine Economy 2.0. No more confusion and hassle!

Top Security

We are using strong public cryptography and the best industry practices. We assure that your money is safe by ensuring your private keys never leave your device and that you have ultimate control over them!

Convenient Backup

All your coins are accessed with one Hierarchical Deterministic Wallet (BIP44), so instead of having one backup file for every coin, you have one master key that you could write on a piece of paper. Never lose access again!
